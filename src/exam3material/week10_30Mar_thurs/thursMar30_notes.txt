More definitions.


A number r is *rational* if r = p/q for some integers p and q, where q ≠ 0.


Suppose a and b are integers. We say that a *divides* b,
written a | b, if b = ac for some c ∈ Z.
In this case we also say that a is a *divisor* of b, and that b is a *multiple* of a.
